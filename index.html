Description of the Power Nine cards

Ancestral Recall: Draw 3 cards or force opponent to draw 3 cards.

Timetwister: Set Timetwister aside in a new graveyard pile.

Shuffle your hand, library, and graveyard together into a new library and draw a new hand of seven cards, leaving all cards in play where they are, opponent must do the same.

Time Walk: Take an extra turn after this one.

Mox Emerald: Add 1 green mana to your mana pool.

Tapping this artifact can be played as an interrupt.

Mox Jet: Add 1 black mana to your mana pool.

Tapping this artifact can be played as an interrupt.

Mox Pearl: Add 1 white mana to your mana pool.

Tapping this artifact can be played as an interrupt.

Mox Ruby: Add 1 red mana to your mana pool.

Tapping this artifact can be played as an interrupt.

Mox Sapphire: Add 1 blue mana to your mana pool.

Tapping this artifact can be played as an interrupt.

Black Lotus: Adds 3 mana of any single color of your choice to your mana pool,

then is discarded. Tapping this artifact can be played as an interrupt.

"In Magic: The Gathering, the Power Nine are nine rare cards found in the Alpha, Beta, and Unlimited sets, and are widely considered to be the most powerful cards ever printed."

-- MTG Wiki
